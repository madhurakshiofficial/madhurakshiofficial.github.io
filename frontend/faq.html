FAQs

Everything you need to know before placing your order.

How to order?

DM us on Instagram with your selected design.

Do you offer customization?

Yes, we assist with sizing and customization.

What is the delivery time?

Usually 5–10 working days depending on design.

Do you ship all over India?

Yes, we deliver across India.

Can I get help with size selection?

Yes, we guide you to choose the perfect size.

What payment methods are available?

Payment details will be shared via Instagram DM.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled only before dispatch.

Will the product look exactly like the picture?

Slight variations may occur due to lighting and fabric texture.

Do you provide a size chart?

Yes, we provide a detailed size chart to help you choose the best fit.

Do you offer custom sizing?

Absolutely. We offer customized sizing for all our designs.

How does the fit of your outfits run?

Most outfits are true to size. Minor alterations can be made if needed.

Are your outfits ready-to-wear or made-to-order?

All pieces are made-to-order to ensure quality and precision.
