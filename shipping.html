A journey handled with care, from us to you.

Your Order Journey

1

Order Confirmation

Once your order is placed, you’ll receive a confirmation — a quiet assurance that your piece is now in motion.

2

Careful Preparation

Each piece is thoughtfully inspected, finished, and wrapped in our signature packaging.

3

Your order is handed over to our delivery partner. Tracking details will be shared with you.

Delivered to You

A Few Things to Keep in Mind

  • ✦ We deliver across most pin codes in India.
  • ✦ Timelines may vary due to external conditions.
  • ✦ For international orders, feel free to contact us.
  • ✦ Please report damaged packages within 24 hours.
  • ✦ Same-day delivery is not available.
